Our Story

Since 2002, our company has been specializing in re-upholstering and restoring upholstered furniture. Clients turn to us to revive their beloved furniture, integrate them into modern designs, and give them a second life. Our professional services include crafting covers, furniture upholstery, replacing fillings, spring blocks, frame timber, or mechanisms.

Our experienced team will go above and beyond to ensure your satisfaction. We conduct all work in our workshop for optimal customization while adhering to deadlines. Utilizing modern materials with proven quality to suit any taste, and offering manufacturer prices. We handle your furniture with care during transportation, so you don't have to worry about cargo logistics; this service is provided free of charge.

We cater to all clients, serving Kyiv and neighboring cities. We are an optimistic team with extensive experience and expertise, tackling tasks that may seem impossible. Your restored favorite furniture will continue to bring comfort and coziness to your home or office.
